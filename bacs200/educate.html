TED Talks

Nardwuar - Do It Yourself!

In this TED Talk, famous interviewer and journaloist "Nardwuar" talks about his journey into talking with famous musicians and politicians. This is one of my favorite talks because he demonstrates the power of being unafraid of manifesting the things you want to do.

Main Lessons:

  • Create oppurtunities for yourself!
  • Don't give up on goals, even if they seem impossible.
  • Ask for the things you want! You will be surprised how far you get.

Scott Mescudi - Hi, I'm Scott

This TED Talk is performed by one of my favorite artists, AKA Kid Cudi. He talks about the struggles of his early childhood and gives a glimpse of his incredible journey to stardom. It is very inspiring to see someone so unapologeticlly themselves.

Main Lessons:

  • Don't let anyone tell you what you can or can't do.
  • Don't anybody stop you from being who you are.
  • Take advantages of the connections you make and network with people you meet.

Anil Seth - Your brain hallucinates your conscious reality

This TED Talk is vastlty different than the other two. The speaker is a neuroscientist who claims that what we call reality is just one big hallucination!

Main Lessons:

  • Reality might not be what it seems.
  • I hope you don't mind having a mild existential crisis...
  • It is fun to deeply consider what the world around us really is.
