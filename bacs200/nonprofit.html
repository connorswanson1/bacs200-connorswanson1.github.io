The Colorado Refuge Skatepark

Finding community, faith, and love through skateboarding.

Vision

The Refuge is an indoor skatepark in Greeley, Colorado, ran by Northern Colorado Youth for Christ. Their mission is to offer you a place to do what you love, regardless of weather or time of year, while building a relationship with faith and creating a sense of community/belonging.

Strategies to achieving this vision:

  1. The Refuge offers free skate sessions for different ages every Friday night. This means that no one will be left out because they couldn't afford it.
  2. The Refuge also offers private skate lessons for beginners that might feel nervous about jumping in the action with others.
  3. During free sessions, workers and volunteers at the park give short, but meaningful talks and discussions about faith and the overall message of the organization.

How you can get involved:

There are a few different ways to get involved!

Volunteering: There are tons of different kinds of volunteers that The Refuge looks for. They look for volunteers that can help in the following areas:

  • Working with kids
  • Event Support
  • Leadership
  • Administrative

If you are interested, please send an email to Jeff Neel, jeff@coloradorefuge.com
